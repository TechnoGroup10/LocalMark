Frequently Asked Questions

How do I change my account email?

You can log in to your account and change it from your Profile > Edit Profile. Then go to the general tab to change your email.

What should I do if my payment fails?

If your payment fails, you can use the COD (Cash on Delivery) option, if available. If your payment is debited from your account within 7-10 days, it will be credited back.

What is your cancellation policy?

You can cancel an order before it is packed/shipped status. Any amount paid will be credited into the same payment mode used during payment.

How do I check order delivery status?

Please go to the 'My Orders' section under the main menu of App/Web/M-site to check your order status.

What is Instant Refunds?

Upon successfully pickup of the return product at your doorstep, Mytra will instantly initiate the refund to your source account or chosen method of refund. Instant Refund is not available for a few select pin codes and for all self-ship returns.

How do I apply a coupon on my order?

You can apply a coupon on the cart page before order placement. The complete list of your saved and valid coupons will be available in the 'My Coupons' tab of App/Web/M-site.
